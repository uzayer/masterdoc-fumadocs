These projects span three contexts — academic research infrastructure, organisational systems for remote teams, and standalone experiments. Most of them share a common thread: I needed something that didn't exist, so I built it.

The structure in each entry follows **What**, **Why**, and **Additional Info**. The why tends to be the interesting part.

## Organisational Systems

Systems built for [Dear Asian Youth](https://dearasianyouth.org/) and South Breeze School, primarily addressing knowledge decay, credit attribution, and scaling remote teams.

| Project | Status | Summary |
| ------- | ------ | ------- |
| [Breezer's Brew](/docs/projects/organisational-systems#breezers-brew-school-student-lit-org) | Completed | South Breeze School's first literary organisation. Published a magazine, raised BDT 140k+ via letterman jackets to cover printing costs. |
| [Literature Workflow Redesign with Ghost CMS](/docs/projects/organisational-systems#literature-organisation-workflow-redesign-with-ghost-cms) | Completed | Migrated DAY Literature from a multi-tab Google Sheets workflow to Ghost CMS, restructuring articles as state objects and editors as first-class citizens. |
| [DAY Design System & Marketing Funnel](/docs/projects/organisational-systems#day-design-system--marketing-funnel) | Ongoing | shadcn/ui-based design system for DAY + a user journey map to treat recruitment and engagement as a measurable funnel. |
| [GitLab-Inspired Handbook](/docs/projects/organisational-systems#gitlab-inspired-handbook) | Ongoing | Public, version-controlled handbook for DAY — documentation as code, with `.codeowners` files enforcing maintenance responsibility per department. |
| [Fieldkit OSS](/docs/projects/organisational-systems#fieldkit-oss) | Ongoing | An opinionated, reproducible tech stack for nonprofits and student organisations. DAY is the live proving ground. |
