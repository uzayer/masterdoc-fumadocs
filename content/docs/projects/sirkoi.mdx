*2024* · Exited

## What

- A startup founded by friends at Colby and Pomona, originally a Rate My Professor for Bangladesh.
- I named it, designed the logo, created the user journey, did the initial UI mockups in Figma, and built the website.
- All of this was done pre-AI, hand-coded.

## Why I left

- Only two universities in Bangladesh (NSU and BRAC) actually let students choose their professors — the model didn't map onto how the rest of the country's universities work, and I didn't think the market was there yet.
- I left to focus on other work rather than keep building around that gap.
- The team later explored an AI-assisted approach to generating IAL/IB study question banks. My instinct was that generating exam-quality questions needs real pedagogical expertise — I'd have leaned toward a curated question database with AI matching content to student weaknesses rather than generating questions outright. That product-judgment instinct is one I've come back to since, in how I think about [LabKit](/projects/labkit) and [ResearchNet](/projects/researchnet).
